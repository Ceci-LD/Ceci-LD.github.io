About Me

Hi! I'm Tsvetelin, a game programmer and software engineering student from Bulgaria, and I've been making games since 13 and playing games from an even younger age. I published my first mobile game on Google Play in August, 2019. Currently I am in the last semester of my Software Engineering Bachelor program at VIA University College in Denmark.

Here you can see some of the projects I have had the opportunity to contribute to, and some personal projects that I have created myself.

Personal Information

Since I was 9 years old I have never lived in a single country more than 2 consecutive years. I was born in Serbia, although my parents are Bulgarian, and have lived in Bulgaria, South Africa, The United States, Greece, Denmark, and Jordan. This has taught me to constantly adapt to changes and has led me to become more open minded and empathetic.

If you feel like you need someone to talk to, you can hit me up to discuss anything, from game development, programming and emerging technologies, to life itself!

Work

I currently do not have any professional experience besides my internship during the 5th semester of my Bachelor program at VIA University College.

I had an opportunity to begin working for a software engineering company after my internship, but decided to focus on game development and work on a personal project.

Internship

  • Position: Software Engineering Intern
  • Company: SevenGate LLC.
  • Place: Amman, Jordan
  • Period: July, 2018 - December, 2018

VIA University College allows Software Engineering students to take an internship during their 5th semester, which is why I took the opportunity to move and work in Amman, Jordan.
During my internship I was in charge with executing delegated tasks from customers on time and with a high quality. I mainly focused on network security and internet protocols. I completed the internship training early and ahead of time, but decided to extend my training period for one more month. Throughout this period, I received two Sophos XG Firewall certiﬁcates, which can be found below.
